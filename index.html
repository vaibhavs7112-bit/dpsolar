About Us

Established in 2018 by Dr. Ravi Prakash Sharma, DP Solar has grown into a trusted name in solar solutions. With over 7 years of experience, we specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial solar systems. Our team ensures top-quality installation, maintenance, and support services.
